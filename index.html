About the Academy

AN Badminton Academy is a dedicated training centre for players who want to learn badminton the right way. Our coaching approach focuses on strong fundamentals, structured practice, physical fitness, and match confidence.

Whether you are a beginner or an aspiring competitive player, our programs are designed to help you improve step by step in a positive and motivating environment.

Coaching Programs

Foundation Program

For beginners (kids & adults) to learn basic strokes, grip, footwork, rules, and on-court movement.

Skill Development Program

For intermediate players focusing on technique improvement, drills, consistency, and game awareness.

Advanced Performance Program

High-intensity training for competitive players preparing for tournaments and match play.

Fitness & Footwork Training

Strength, agility, stamina, and movement training designed specifically for badminton players.
